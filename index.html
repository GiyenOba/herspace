Our Story

Empowering entrepreneurs through community and innovation

Where We Are Now

The legacy baton for Her Power Space (HPS) was passed on in May 2024 when a few friends, then existing customers of HPS, agreed to purchase the business from its previous owner and founder Michelle Talbert Esq.

Kadiann, Ashergay and Patsy formed Gold KAP Management Inc. and purchased Her Power Space. The trio now turned duo of Kadiann & Patsy are actively building on the legacy of Michelle, by strengthening the foundation and ensuring that the residents of the city of Lauderhill and surrounding communities have access to the necessary resources for starting, and growing their own successful business.

How We Started

Her Power Space is a Woman forward, Men welcoming boutique co-working space, business event venue, and podcast studio, founded by recovering corporate attorney Michelle Y. Talbert.

We exist to serve the business needs of our community, especially side hustlers and solopreneurs, who often feel isolated and lack access to resources for success.

We opened our doors on January 12, 2020 and, due to the pandemic, on March 31, 2020 Michelle locked the doors and wouldn't return to the Space until May.

What a first year!
